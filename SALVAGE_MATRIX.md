# Salvage Matrix

As of March 29, 2026.

This matrix is about mechanism reallocation, not score transfer. The invalid historical `Conker` numbers do not survive as legal causal frontier claims; what survives is the architectural lesson and where it belongs now.

## What Conker Keeps

- the control lesson from `Conker-5/7`
- the claim that very small structural surfaces can dominate model behavior
- the requirement that any rebuilt mechanism must stay causal, normalized, and audit-clean

## What Conker Does Not Keep

- the raw `~0.55 / 0.53` rows as public frontier numbers
- the `Conker-6` `~0.072` mask row as a causal score
- any gain that depended on diagonal or upper-triangle leakage

## Reallocation Read

- `BLINX` owns the noncausal recoverability signal.
- `giddy-up` owns the translation from noncausal discovery to legal causal hints.
- `Conker` owns only the part that still works under strict causal audit.
