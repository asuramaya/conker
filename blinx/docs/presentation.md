# Presentation

This page is the shortest current read on `blinx`.

Use it when you want the codec line and what it means without stepping through the inherited tree.

## What BLINX Is

`BLINX` is the noncausal lossless-compression and oracle-discovery line. It is not a causal `bpb` submission path; its value is as a structure probe, codec lab, and teacher surface.

## Active Branches

- `BLINX-4`: control-first codec mutation
- `BLINX-5`: payload-first codec mutation
- `BLINX-5a/b/c`: payload ablations
- `BLINX-6`: dictionary factoring
- `BLINX-7`: typed pruning

## Core Finding

- on ordinary repo text, side-data economics still dominate
- BLINX only starts to win clearly on cleaner recursive synthetic structure
- the branch is most useful as a probe, not yet as a practical general compressor
